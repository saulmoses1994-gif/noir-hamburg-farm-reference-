## Suggested tightening patterns

The vast majority of long titles follow one of these bloated patterns. Trim to these compact versions to bring everything under 65:

| Pattern (bloated) | Suggested pattern (compact) |
|---|---|
| `Escort {Area} — Premium Begleitung in {Area} \| Noir Hamburg` | `Escort {Area} — Premium Begleitung \| Noir Hamburg` |
| `Escort {Area} — Premium Companionship in {Area} \| Noir Hamburg` | `Escort {Area} — Premium Companion \| Noir Hamburg` |
| `{Service} Hamburg \| {long descriptor} \| Noir Hamburg` | `{Service} Hamburg \| Noir Hamburg` |
| `Magazine — Noir Hamburg \| Lifestyle, Hamburg Guide & Travel Recommendations` | `Noir Magazine — Lifestyle & Hamburg Guide` |
| `Hamburg Areas — Premium Escort in der ganzen Metropolregion \| Noir Hamburg` | `Hamburg Areas — Premium Escort \| Noir Hamburg` |
| Long blog titles like `Elbphilharmonie — Planning the Perfect Concert Evening 2026 \| Noir Hamburg` | Drop trailing brand OR the "2026" year OR shorten descriptor |

## How to apply the changes

- **Service pages**: Edit each Service in `Admin → Services → {slug} → SEO → Meta title`.
- **Area pages**: Edit each Area in `Admin → Areas → {slug} → SEO → Meta title`.
- **Blog posts**: Edit in `Admin → Blog → {slug} → Meta title`.
- **Static pages** (`/blog`, `/areas`, `/escort-hamburg`): update the fallback title in the corresponding page.js / component (`app/(de)/blog/page.js`, `app/(en)/en/blog/page.js`, `components/public/AreasBody.js`, `components/public/EscortHamburgBody.js`, etc.).

Once titles are updated in the CMS, no redeploy is needed — pages regenerate on next request (`dynamic = 'force-dynamic'`).
